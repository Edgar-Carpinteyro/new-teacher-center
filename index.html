New Teacher Center

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower teachers and school leaders to create the most effective learning environment for students. We provide resources and training to help educators and students succeed.

The Problem

A new wave of young and motivated teachers enters the workforce every year. However, without the proper preparation, a high percentage of new teachers will be affected by burnout. Here are the top 4 reasons:

  1. Lack of support from leadership and colleagues
  2. Lack of sufficient resources
  3. Insufficient training
  4. Evaluation processes is outdated

The Solution

When it comes to teacher training, we've got an outstanding reputation. Here is how we help schools across Los Angeles.

Outstanding Professional Development

Professional development for new and experienced teachers

World-Class Instruction Support

Year-long instructional-based support in the classroom

Individualized Coaching

Pairing new teachers with experienced teachers in a mentorship system

Dependable Resources

Provides a database of resources for teachers to access
